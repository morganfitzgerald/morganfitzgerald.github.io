Projects

I am passionate about understanding how our heart influences our brain.

I probe the connection between heart function and brain activity, focusing on how the heart's signals, particularly the shape of the heartbeat wave and the '1/f signal', impact neural dynamics. This exploration is particularly aimed at understanding the neural activity associated with cognitive abilities like memory and attention. My goal is to uncover how age-realted changes in heart signals affect working memory.

This research underscores the importance of heart health in cognitive function, offering new insights into aging and cognitive health by linking the condition of the heart with brain performance.
