Location
========

Our labs are located in two locations on the main campus at UC San Diego in La Jolla, CA. We occupy a large space in the Cognitive Science Building (CSB) as well as offices in the [Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute](https://datascience.ucsd.edu/).

Given the lab’s diverse range of interests, we collaborate with colleagues all around the world.
